Customers Segmentation Project.

October 2023


Brief

This project involved the querying of data from the TravelTide database to study customers behavior and to assign designated perks to customers of the TravelTide organisation. Customers with activities in the database of the organisation was studied. Customers with specific behaviors were designated for perks designed by the organisation's new Marketing Head. These perks were aimed at keep customers engaged with the organisation.

The project involved the use of several data analytics techniques to gain insights on customers behavior and enablement for perks. The project also involved the used of the SQL and Python Programming languages for a better analysis of the insights.
